Course Overview

A three-year program designed to meet the needs and demands of those aspirants who are looking forward to making their career in advertising, journalism, public relations, education, social work, and more. This course provides specialization in the discipline of:

  • Hindi
  • English
  • Sociology
  • Political science
  • History

ELIGIBILITY

Applicants must have completed 10+2 from a recognized central or state board with a minimum of 45% marks.

ADMISSION PROCEDURE

The eligible candidate must get themselves registered in Samarth Portal https://sdsuv.samarth.ac.in/index.php/site/login followed by counseling based on merit.

CAREER PROSPECTS

The program opens a plethora of opportunities for students in diverse fields, extending its relevance to more generic career paths such as Civil Services, Armed Forces, Banking, NGOs, and Social Services. With exposure to numerous subjects, learners can opt for careers in varied fields such as HR, Marketing, Advertising, IT, Healthcare, and more.
