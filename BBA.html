BBA

Course Overview

A three-year regular program designed to meet the needs and demands of those aspirants who are looking forward to making their career in corporate world or willing to own business. This course provides specialization in the areas of:

  • Marketing
  • Finance
  • Human Resource Management
  • International Business

ELIGIBILITY

Applicants must have completed 10+2 from a recognized central or state board with a minimum of 45% marks.

ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Admission to our BBA program is facilitated either through the CUET scorecard or via merit-based counseling conducted by the college.

CAREER PROSPECTS

Students can groom themselves to become successful managers in diverse businesses sector such as FMCG, Consumer durable, Banking, Insurance, Telecom, Media, Real Estate, Automobiles, and others.
