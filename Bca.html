BCA or Bachelor of omputer Applications is an undergraduate Information Technology course. Computer Applications or Application software is computer software designed to help the user to perform singular or multiple related specific tasks. The duration of Bachelor of Computer Applications is three years, divided into a number of semesters which are generally six in number. The course provides sound practical skills addressing problems which arise from computer systems and applications. Owing to the wide popularity amongst the students various Computer Science Schools have come up offering Computer Science graduate programs at par with B.C.A. B.C.A. can also be pursued through Distance Learning Programs by various Institutions.B.C.A is a degree which marks you equivalent to IT professionals as from best BCA college in dehradun.

Students have option to grow in one of the following field

  • Cloud computing
  • Ethical Hacking
  • GIS
  • Software Developing
  • Software testing
  • Networking
  • IT Support

The Programme aims to provide learners with an ability to prepare for Job Placement, successful career and to become Leaders for comprehensive development of the society. These aims are achieved with:

  • Understanding the learners Goal and choice of Industry and the Job.
  • An introduction to the study of computer application.
  • A progressively higher level of understanding and critical awareness of the main issues in Technology management
  • A global holistic perspective of management and computer application.
  • A range of intellectual skill and technical competencies required for effective management.
  • The opportunity to link the program of computer application with study of an advanced level of a specialist pathway.
  • Applying and converting the above understanding into application and learning
