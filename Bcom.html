Honours Bachelor of Commerce

The honours Bachelor of Commerce B.Com. (Hons) is a 3-year commerce degree in business. It may consist of a four-year programe or of a one-year program taken subsequent to a three year bachelors degree. The degree requires additional academic courses to be completed with higher academic performance standards and may also require a researched thesis component. The Honours Bachelor of Commerce degree also serves as an abridgement between the undergraduate program and postgraduate programs,including the Master of Commerce (M.Com. or M.Comm.) and the Master of Business Administration (MBA) degrees. The subsequent one-year of study is often focused exclusively on a single subject-area.
