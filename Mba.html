MBA

Course Overview

Nimbus provides the best- in class MBA.Approved by AICTE , New Delhi, Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India. Affiliated to Uttarakhand Technical University ,Dehradun,(Uttarakhand)

A two year full time regular program designed to meet the needs and demands of forward looking and progressive corporate sectors in India and abroad . NAM is amongst the Top MBA, Management colleges in Dehradun, Uttarakhand and North India. This course has specialization in the following areas.

  • Marketing
  • Finance
  • Human resource management
  • International business
  • Information technology

Advantages or Benefits of Master of Business Administration Degree:-

MBA and other programs in Management are highly demanding in the present scene because of the emergence of the large corporate houses, MNCs and industrial giants and the world wide economic growth. The social and individualistic view point is change largely, were people are more concentrated on Career growth and maximize the earning rather than job security.The employment opportunities doubles from getting education from best management college in Dehradun with Nimbus

MBA boosts individuals passion and hunger for success

  • Acts as a Career Accelerator
  • Develops the quality of Leadership and ladder to entrepreneurship
  • Add value to the competency

The Programme aims to provide learners with an ability to prepare for Job Placement, successful career and to become Leaders for comprehensive development of the society. These aims are achieved with:

  1. Understanding the learners Goal and choice of Industry and the Job.
  2. An introduction to the study of business and management.
  3. A progressively higher level of understanding and critical awareness of the main issues in strategic management.
  4. A global holistic perspective of management and business administration.
  5. A range of intellectual skill and management competencies required for effective management practice.
  6. The opportunity to link the programme of general management studies with study of an advanced level of a specialist pathway which complements and broadens understanding of business and management.
  7. Applying and converting the above understanding into application and learning
