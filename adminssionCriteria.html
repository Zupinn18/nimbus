Admission Criteria

Welcome to NIMBUS ACADEMY OF MANAGEMENT!

Our institution is dedicated to fostering a diverse and inclusive community of learners who are passionate about their academic career and eager to make a difference in the world. We offer a variety of courses including B.A., B. Com, BCA, BBA, MBA, and B.Ed. Our admission process is designed to identify talented individuals who will thrive in our dynamic academic environment. Connect with us to learn more about our transparent admission requirements and start your journey with us!

Course Admission Procedure Eligiblity Criteria
MBA The eligible candidates must get themselves registered at the institute which will followed by counselling based on merit. Graduation with 50% marks (at any stream), CAT /MAT/ GMAT
BCA Admission to our BCA program is facilitated either through the CUET scorecard or via merit-based counseling conducted by the college. 10+2 with Mathematics as a compulsory subject from a recognized Board with a minimum 45% marks.
BBA Admission to our BBA program is facilitated either through the CUET scorecard or via merit-based counseling conducted by the college. Applicants must have completed 10+2 from a recognized central or state board with a minimum of 45% marks.
B.Com The eligible candidate must get themselves registered in Samarth Portal followed by counseling based on merit. Applicants must have completed 10+2 from a recognized board with a minimum of 45% marks.
B.A. The eligible candidate must get themselves registered in Samarth Portal followed by counseling based on merit. Applicants must have completed 10+2 from a recognized board with a minimum of 45% marks.
B.Ed. Qualified candidates of Uttarakhand State Combined B.Ed. Entrance Test undergo counseling for admissions, with registration on the Samarth portal being mandatory. Graduate with at least50% marks for general category and 45% marks for SC &ST category along
How to Apply
  • Application for NAM can be filled in both mode online and offline.
  • Online application can be filled by downloading the application form from the website of the college.
  • Offline application can be filled by visiting the college campus.
  • Candidates need to mention the personal details, contacts etc. Without any error as the college authority can contact the candidate if required
