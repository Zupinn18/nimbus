About Us

NIMBUS is one of the premier leading Management institutes in Uttarakhand, established in 1998 under the management of Doon Educational Trust Society (DETS), which comprises eminent educationists. Our logo carries a proclamation in Sanskrit: “विद्याविनियोगात समृद्धि” which means “Fostering prosperity through the power of education.”

Our aspiration is to become a leading academic institution dedicated to fostering excellence in management, innovation, and entrepreneurship for the betterment of both business and society. NIMBUS has been active in the fields of Management and Education for more than two decades. It has established its name among the top business schools in Uttarakhand. Apart from classroom teaching, Nimbus has strong industry tie-ups to benefit students in gaining practical knowledge of business processes. The best learning methodologies have shaped the students in such a way that various companies regularly visit the campus for placements.
