Multipurpose Hall

    Auditorium is a versatile concert hall renowned for its exceptional acoustics and welcoming ambiance. It serves as an optimal venue for a wide array of events, both academic and non-academic, including lectures, conferences, musical performances, dance shows. The facility is equipped with a comprehensive range of audiovisual equipment, including a powerful projector specially imported for multiple uses. This high-quality projector supports various functions, from displaying slides to screening high-resolution videos and motivational content, further enhancing the audience's experience.
