Placement Report

Nimbus Academy of Management Dehradun have triumphantly scored 70 Percent Placement for the graduating year (2022-24). A total of 151 entities participated and 46 entities got pre-placement offers. Dr. Rajesh Tiwari congratulated and appreciated all the students for this achievement. He said “we take immense pride in nurturing students poised to assume significant leadership roles within India’s corporate landscape as conscientious leaders”. It brings us immense joy to declare that, against the backdrop of challenging market dynamics, NAM has accomplished 70% placement with resounding success.
